Hello, I'm Xiaohan Zhang

I am a passionate Bayern Munich fan who enjoys following their games and celebrating their victories. Currently residing in Canada, I am dedicated to advancing my knowledge in cybersecurity and digital forensics.

As a computer enthusiast, I love building game desktops and staying up-to-date with the latest hardware trends. Additionally, I have a deep appreciation for Audi cars, always eager to learn about the latest models and technological advancements.

In my free time, I enjoy playing soccer and collecting soccer cards, particularly rare and valuable ones. I am also a cat lover and cherish the moments spent with my furry friends.

My academic journey led me to the British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT), where I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Forensic Investigation with a specialization in Digital Forensics and Cybersecurity. I also hold a diploma in Computer Information System Administration from BCIT.

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my interests and experiences. If you have any questions or just want to connect, don't hesitate to reach out!
